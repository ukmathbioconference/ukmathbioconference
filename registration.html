Register for the Conference

We have limited on-campus accommodation and space restrictions, so early registration is recommended. We have very limited scholarships, prioritising early career researchers. In the registration form, please only select this option if you do not have access to other funding sources, as we would like to enable wide participation. The standard registration fee is £250, and £150 for PhD students.
